Augur



How To Use This App

Augur helps photographers prep for a shoot by providing important information about the venue and weather. Enter the date, name of the venue, and venue address. Augur works best within 30 days of your scheduled photoshoot.

Augur then returns the time of sunrise & sunset, general weather information for the day, and images of the venue from the web. Augur also gives you the option to redirect to a Google Streetview of the provided address.
